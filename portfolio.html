About Me

Welcome to my about page! My name is Yatinder Singh, and I'm currently a first-year Computer Science Engineering (CSE) student at Chitkara University. I am deeply passionate about technology and its potential to drive positive change in the world. From an early age, I've been fascinated by computers and their ability to solve complex problems, which led me to pursue a career in the field of computer science. At Chitkara University, I'm actively involved in exploring various aspects of computer science, ranging from programming and software development to artificial intelligence and machine learning. I believe in the power of continuous learning and strive to expand my knowledge and skills with each passing day.

Apart from academics, I enjoy engaging in extracurricular activities and contributing to the vibrant student community at Chitkara University. Whether it's participating in hackathons, organizing tech events, or collaborating on projects with my peers, I'm always eager to learn and grow both personally and professionally.
